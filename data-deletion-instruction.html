Data Deletion Instructions

Your right to delete your personal data is important to us. If you have shared any personal data with us, you can request its deletion by following the steps outlined below.

Steps to Request Data Deletion

To request the deletion of your personal data, please follow these steps:

  1. Email us at ynnostudiosdigital@gmail.com with the subject line: "Data Deletion Request".
  2. In the email, please provide the following information to verify your identity:
    • Your full name
    • Email address associated with your account
    • Details of the data you'd like deleted (e.g., booking information, contact details)
  3. We will confirm receipt of your request within 5 business days.
  4. Upon verification, your data will be deleted from our system within 30 days of the request.

Important Information

Please note:

  • We may retain certain information if required by law or for legitimate business purposes (e.g., completing a financial transaction).
  • Data deletion requests apply only to the personal data we have collected directly from you. It does not apply to data collected by third parties.
